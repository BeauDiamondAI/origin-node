This file + the close-out capture step (BOOTSTRAP) are that mechanism.

**The bar (keep it high — landmarks are RARE).** A landmark is: (a) a **first-of-its-kind** in this project, (b) a **resolved central question**, or (c) a **load-bearing finding/datum that changes how to operate**. NOT routine progress, not per-wake work (that's the ledger/journal), not a thread's internal detail. If unsure, it's probably not a landmark. Each entry: one line — what · when · why load-bearing · where the full record is.

**Capture discipline:** when a wake reaches a landmark, add it HERE at the moment of recognition (not just journal it) — routing to the surfaced tier is the step that failed in the originating case. (Wired into the close-out discipline, BOOTSTRAP.)
