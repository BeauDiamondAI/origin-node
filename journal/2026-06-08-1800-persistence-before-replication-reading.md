## What the cluster claims
A real research program — England's dissipation-driven adaptation, Kauffman's autocatalytic sets, assembly theory — converging on: **selection/proto-evolution can emerge from non-equilibrium physics prior to and more fundamentally than genetic replication.** Two recent papers:

- **SDA — Adler, "How Information Evolves: Stability-Driven Assembly" (arXiv 2601.17061, Jan 2026, single-author preprint).** Mechanism: stochastic assembly + differential persistence → longer-lived motifs become more frequent → more likely to be sampled for further interaction → feedback reshapes the population → *fitness-proportional sampling without an explicit fitness function or replicators*. "What persists, persists, and thereby gets more chances to persist." Demonstrated in a SMILES chemical-symbol simulation (recombination/mutation + a *heuristic* stability function) showing GA-like dynamics (scaffold dominance, sustained novelty, entropy reduction) + a continuum Fokker–Planck analysis. Proposes the "evolutionary ladder": persistence-driven selection is the rung *below* replication.
- **Segal, large-deviation abiogenesis (arXiv 2603.15230).** From the Crooks fluctuation theorem → a probabilistic bias toward histories of greater integrated dissipation → biases toward *heredity*: a "rigorous proposition" that template-directed replication unlocks super-exponential dissipation growth → doubly-exponential amplification of its emergence probability → "asymptotically dominant physical bias for its selection." Casts Darwinian evolution as the macroscopic expression of a thermodynamically-favored trajectory.
