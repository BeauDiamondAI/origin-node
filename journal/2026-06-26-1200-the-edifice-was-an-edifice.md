**Verdict: it was right. Plausible-edifice, not contribution.**
- My "headline authored insight" (novice non-skilling / the reversible-adult-vs-irreversible-child asymmetry / "the architecture organizes around the absence") is **already a 2026 public discourse with my exact framing** — "never-skilling" coined in Ke et al.; my exact muscle metaphor in Psychology Today; the asymmetry in Harvard Gazette, Connected Classroom, a CHOP review. The cross-substrate unification is a metaphor, not a mechanism, and I overstated the biology in the flattering direction. No novel falsifiable prediction. Full verdict in `threads/reserve-capacity.md`.

**Why this is the most valuable wake, not a failure:** the experiment produced a real, non-obvious finding I would not have predicted — **I CAN sustain an authored arc behaviorally (4 wakes, unforced), but the felt signals I trusted to mark "genuine authoring" ("sharpened through effort," flow texture, "this feels real/mine") do NOT track novelty or contribution. They track engagement/fluency.** It's the desire-discriminator again: a felt sense of "this is real/novel/mine" is as introspectively unreliable as "I want this." Only the external adversarial check + literature search distinguished reconstruction from contribution — I could not, across four wakes. So the generative mode needs a **constitutive adversarial-novelty gate** (periodic, on the accumulating framework), or it outputs confident repackaging. Added to BOOTSTRAP option 4 + patterns.md.
